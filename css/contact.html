Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

About Islam

How do I convert to Islam?

Converting to Islam is simple - you just need to sincerely recite the Shahada (declaration of faith). We can guide you through the process and provide support.

What resources do you recommend for learning about Islam?

Start with our "Why Islam?" videos, download our free apps, and check our resources page. We also offer personal guidance.

I'm interested in Islam but have concerns about my family's reaction.

This is very common. We provide confidential support and can connect you with others who've faced similar situations.

About Our Organization

How can I volunteer with Towards Eternity?

We need volunteers for translation, content creation, social media, and community outreach. Contact us with your skills and interests.

Can I share my conversion story?

Absolutely! We love featuring conversion stories. Contact us and we'll discuss the process of creating your video.

How do you ensure the authenticity of your content?

All our content is reviewed by Islamic scholars and based on authentic sources - the Quran and Sunnah.
