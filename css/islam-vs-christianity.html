Side-by-Side Comparison

Quick reference guide to key differences

Islam

Concept of God

Tawhid (Oneness): Allah is One, indivisible, without partners or associates. No Trinity.

Jesus (Isa)

Prophet: One of the greatest prophets, born of virgin Mary, performed miracles, but not divine or son of God.

Salvation

Faith + Good Deeds: Belief in Allah and righteous actions. No original sin concept.

Scripture

Quran: Final revelation, preserved in original Arabic, unchanged since revelation.

Christianity

Concept of God

Trinity: God exists as three persons - Father, Son (Jesus), and Holy Spirit - yet one God.

Jesus Christ

Son of God: Divine, second person of Trinity, died for humanity's sins, resurrected.

Salvation

Faith in Jesus: Salvation through belief in Jesus' sacrifice. Grace, not works.

Scripture

Bible: Old and New Testament, various translations, compiled over centuries.
